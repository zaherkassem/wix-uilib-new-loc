The label and tab are connected by label's "for" attribute and tab's "name" attribute. So the first tabs.label for="example" in the tabs.header will be the label to the tab in the tabs.content with name="example". is a special component which hides all tabs.labels following it in markup. After the click on it the hidden tabs.labels will be shown and tabs.showMoreLabel will be hidden. tabs.showMoreLabel doesn't impact the connections between tabs.labels and tabs. All of these three components can contain anything you like. You can add class and style to any of the described components. Provide "class" and "style" properties to the components. There are two layout presets for placing the tabLabels on top and on left of the tabContent. Just provide the "top" or "left" to the baseUI.tabs class property.
